Our Story

Who We Are

Sawa Repairs & Solutions was founded with a simple observation: most Kenyan homeowners were paying premium prices for solar systems they could get at a fraction of the cost — if they had the right partner.

We saw an opportunity to combine construction expertise with solar installation under one roof. While competitors focused on either construction or solar, we built a company that does both — and does them well.

Today we serve homeowners, landlords, developers, and businesses across Nairobi, Kiambu, and the greater central region, with a growing track record of completed projects and satisfied clients.

Every Kenyan home and business deserves access to quality construction and affordable clean energy — without paying premium prices for a brand name.
