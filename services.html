01 — Featured Service

Solar Water Heaters

Our most popular service. Pressurized solar water heating systems from Ultrasun, Chromagen, Kwikot & Solahart that eliminate or drastically reduce the cost of heating water for your home, rental, hotel, or institution.

A solar water heater typically pays for itself within 2–3 years through KPLC savings alone. After that, it's essentially free hot water for 15–25 years.

With a 300L system, most families cut their monthly KPLC bill by up to 60%. Rental landlords often see complete ROI within 18 months.
