FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started. Still have questions? WhatsApp us directly — we answer fast.

Ask on WhatsApp
How much does a solar water heater cost in Kenya?

Prices vary by size: 150L systems start from KES 45,000, 200L from KES 58,000, and 300L from KES 85,000 — all including professional installation. Contact us for a site-specific quote.

How long does installation take?

Most solar water heater installations are completed in a single day. Larger backup power systems typically take 1–2 days. We do a site visit first to confirm the timeline.

Do you offer a warranty?

Yes. Solar panels carry a 25-year performance warranty. Inverters and batteries have manufacturer warranties (typically 2–5 years). We provide 1 year workmanship warranty on all installations.

Which areas do you serve?

We serve Nairobi, Kiambu, Ruiru, Thika, Juja, Kikuyu, Limuru, Ngong, Rongai, Westlands, Kasarani, Roysambu and surrounding areas. Contact us for other locations.

Can solar work during cloudy days or at night?

Solar panels generate power even on cloudy days, though at reduced capacity. With a battery backup system, stored energy powers your home at night and during cloudy periods.

Do you do both solar and construction?

Yes — that's our unique advantage. We handle general construction, building repairs, and solar installation under one roof. One company, one contract, zero coordination headaches.
